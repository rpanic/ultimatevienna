About the Program

Two squads, one mission — competing with heart and spirit.

Echo and Rumble form Ultimate Vienna’s men’s open program in collaboration with EÖFC. Echo is the competitive squad, while Rumble is the development team — both share the same values of commitment, dedication, and spirit of the game. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just getting started, there’s a place for you.

Echo

Our competitive men’s open squad. Echo represents Ultimate Vienna at the highest level, competing in national and international tournaments.

Rumble

Our development squad, designed for players who are building their skills and working their way toward competitive play. Rumble offers dedicated coaching staff and focussed on growth and fundamentals.
