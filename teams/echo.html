About the Team

Competing with heart, playing with spirit.

Echo (and our development squad Rumble) is Ultimate Vienna’s men’s open program. We compete in national and international tournaments, pushing each other to grow as athletes and as teammates. Whether you’re a seasoned player or picking up a disc for the first time, there’s a place for you. We value commitment, coachability, and above all — spirit of the game.
