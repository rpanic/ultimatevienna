About the Team

Where young players find their game.

The Foxes are Ultimate Vienna’s youth team, open to players of all skill levels under 20. We focus on developing fundamentals, building confidence on the field, and fostering a love for the game. Our coaches emphasise teamwork, fair play, and most importantly — fun. Whether you want to compete at youth nationals or just throw a disc with friends, the Foxes are for you.
