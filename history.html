Our History

From a handful of players to a club that calls Vienna home.

The Beginning
Ultimate Vienna was founded by a group of ultimate enthusiasts who saw the need for a dedicated club in Austria’s capital. What started as informal throw sessions in the park quickly grew into organised practices and a shared vision: to build a club that could compete at the highest level while staying true to the spirit of the game.
Building Echo
The men’s open team — Echo — was the club’s first competitive squad. With a focus on structured offence and disciplined defence, Echo made its mark at national and regional tournaments. Over time, a development squad — Rumble — was formed alongside Echo to give newer players a pathway into competitive play.
Growing with Foxes
Recognising that the future of the sport depends on the next generation, Ultimate Vienna launched the Foxes — its youth programme. The Foxes brought young players into the ultimate community, teaching fundamentals and fostering a love for the game in a supportive environment.
Today
Ultimate Vienna continues to grow, with both Echo/Rumble and the Foxes training and competing year-round. The club remains committed to its founding principles: competitive excellence, inclusive community, and unwavering spirit of the game. New players are always welcome — whether you’re picking up a disc for the first time or bringing years of experience.
